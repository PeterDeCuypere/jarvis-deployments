E-Beam Processing Terminology

PCN
Process Control Number - unique identifier for each processing job
SPSA
Sterilization Process Setup Agreement - product configuration defining dose limits
kGy
kiloGray - unit of absorbed radiation dose
MeV
Mega electron Volt - beam energy measurement
LINAC
Linear Accelerator - the electron beam source
Dosimeter
Device to measure absorbed radiation dose
Beam Current
Electron flow rate in milliamps (mA)
Conveyor Speed
Product movement speed through beam (m/min)
SAL
Sterility Assurance Level - probability of non-sterile unit
